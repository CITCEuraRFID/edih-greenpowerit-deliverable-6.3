# Acme Robotics Company Handbook

## Company Overview
Acme Robotics was founded in 2018 by Sarah Chen and Michael Torres. The company's headquarters are located in Austin, Texas. Acme Robotics develops autonomous warehouse robots for logistics and supply chain operations.

## Products

### RoverX
RoverX is an autonomous mobile robot released in March 2022. It can carry up to 500 kilograms and operates for 14 hours on a single charge. RoverX uses LiDAR and computer vision for navigation.

### LiftBot Pro
LiftBot Pro was launched in September 2023. It is designed for pallet handling and can lift loads up to 1,500 kilograms. The robot includes collision avoidance software version 4.2.
